# Product Context

## The Narrative
The year is 3147 CE. Humanity has spread to Proxima Centauri b ("Kepler's Promise"). The Terran Expansion Authority (TEA) oversees colonization.
The player controls **Sergeant James Cole (Callsign: SPECTER)**, a "Hell Jumper" marine.
**The Mission**: Find his brother, **Corporal Marcus Cole (Callsign: HAMMER)**, who went missing with Recon Team Vanguard at FOB Delta.

## The Enemy
**Strain-X / "Chitin"**: An insectoid hive species living deep underground. They were awakened by mining operations.
- **Characteristics**: Insectoid, hive mind, bioluminescent, subterranean.
- **Variants**: Drones, Grunts, Brutes, Spitters, and the Queen.

## User Experience Goals
- **Grounded & Human**: A military story about brotherhood, not a generic sci-fi shooter.
- **Visceral Combat**: Heavy weapons, tactical movement, intense firefights.
- **Exploration**: Procedurally generated world that feels alive and dangerous.
- **Persistence**: Changes to the world (destroyed buildings, dead enemies) are saved.

## Design Guidelines
- **Visuals**: Modern military equipment, worn and battle-tested. High contrast lighting.
- **UI**: Utilitarian, functional, "heads-up display" style.
- **Controls**: Optimized for touch (virtual joysticks, screen drag for look).
